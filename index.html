Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Weveloper.org offer?

Weveloper.org specializes in a wide range of web development services, including custom website development using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Next.js, and Tailwind CSS; e-commerce website development; payment gateway integration; implementing Strapi as a headless CMS; SEO services; and WordPress development.

How does Weveloper.org ensure full customer satisfaction?

We pride ourselves on our commitment to customer satisfaction. Our process involves close collaboration with our clients at every step, from initial consultation to final delivery, ensuring the project not only meets but exceeds expectations. We offer revisions and value your feedback to make sure the final product perfectly aligns with your vision.

Are your rates really lower than the industry standard? How do you manage that?

Yes, our rates are competitively priced below industry standards. We manage this by maintaining an efficient, agile development process and keeping overhead costs low, without compromising on the quality of our work. Can Weveloper.org handle high-traffic e-commerce sites?

Absolutely. We have extensive experience in developing robust e-commerce platforms designed to handle high traffic and transactions smoothly. Our solutions are scalable, secure, and tailored to meet the unique needs of your business, ensuring a seamless shopping experience for your customers.

How does Weveloper.org approach SEO services?

Our SEO services are comprehensive, beginning with a thorough analysis of your current website and industry to identify key opportunities. We then tailor a strategy that includes on-page SEO, content creation, keyword optimization, and technical SEO improvements to enhance your visibility and rankings on search engines.

What is a headless CMS, and why does Weveloper.org implement Strapi?

A headless CMS is a back-end content management system where the content storage and delivery are separated from the front-end layer. This architecture allows for greater flexibility, scalability, and speed in web development. We implement Strapi, a leading open-source headless CMS, because it provides a powerful, customizable, and developer-friendly platform that can adapt to any design without limitations.

Does Weveloper.org provide support and maintenance after a project is launched?

Yes, we offer ongoing support and maintenance services to ensure your website continues to perform optimally. Our support includes regular updates, security checks, and technical assistance to address any issues that may arise post-launch.
